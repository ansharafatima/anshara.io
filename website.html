About Five Nights at Freddy's

Five Nights at Freddy's is a popular indie horror game series created by Scott Cawthon. The game is set in Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, a fictional restaurant. Players take on the role of a night security guard who must survive the night shift while being attacked by animatronic characters.

The Games

Five Nights at Freddy's (2014)

The first game in the series introduces players to Freddy Fazbear's Pizza and the animatronics. Players must survive five nights while avoiding Freddy, Bonnie, Chica, and Foxy.

Five Nights at Freddy's 2 (2014)

The second game expands on the first with new characters and mechanics. Players must deal with the original animatronics as well as new ones like Toy Freddy, Toy Bonnie, and Toy Chica.

Five Nights at Freddy's 3 (2015)

The third game is set in a horror attraction based on Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. Players must survive while avoiding Springtrap and hallucinations of the original animatronics.

Five Nights at Freddy's 4 (2015)

The fourth game takes place in a child's bedroom. Players must fend off nightmare versions of the animatronics using a flashlight and listening for audio cues.

Five Nights at Freddy's: Sister Location (2016)

The fifth game introduces a new setting and characters, including Circus Baby and her gang. Players must complete different tasks each night while avoiding the animatronics.

Five Nights at Freddy's: Pizzeria Simulator (2017)

This game combines elements of a tycoon game with the traditional horror mechanics. Players manage their own pizzeria and must survive the night shifts.

Five Nights at Freddy's: Help Wanted (2019)

A virtual reality game that revisits events from the previous games. Players must complete various mini-games while avoiding the animatronics.

Five Nights at Freddy's: Security Breach (2021)

The latest installment features a free-roaming environment and new characters. Players must explore the Mega Pizzaplex and avoid the Glamrock animatronics.
