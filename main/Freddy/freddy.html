Character Overview

Freddy Fazbear is the main antagonist of the Five Nights at Freddy's series. He is an animatronic bear and the mascot of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, where he entertains children with his band during the day. However, at night, Freddy and his fellow animatronics come to life and become dangerous, hunting the night guard who must survive their attacks.

Appearance

Freddy is a brown animatronic bear with a lighter shade of brown on his belly and the insides of his ears. He wears a black top hat and bow tie and holds a microphone in his right hand. His eyes are usually a bright blue, but they turn black with small white pupils when he is in attack mode.

Behavior

Freddy is known for his stealth and his tendency to become more active as the week progresses. Unlike other animatronics, Freddy tends to remain in the shadows and is difficult to track on the cameras. He is also known for his eerie, slow laugh and his musical jingle that plays when he is close to attacking.

Trivia

  • Freddy Fazbear is voiced by Kellen Goff in the later games.
  • Freddy's signature jingle is based on the song "Toreador March" from the opera Carmen.
  • In the first game, Freddy becomes active on Night 3 and is the hardest animatronic to avoid.
  • Freddy has multiple variations across the series, including Golden Freddy, Nightmare Freddy, and Rockstar Freddy.
