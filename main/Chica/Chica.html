Character Overview

Chica the Chicken is a key antagonist in the Five Nights at Freddy's series. She is an animatronic chicken and a backup singer in Freddy Fazbear's band. Chica is known for her unsettling appearance and relentless pursuit of the night guard.

Appearance

Chica is a yellow animatronic chicken with orange beak and feet. She wears a bib with "Let's Eat!!!" written on it. Her eyes are usually round and purple, but they turn black with small white pupils when she is in attack mode.

Behavior

Chica is known for her persistent behavior. She often appears in the right side of the security office and can approach from various locations. Her attacks are consistent, making her a significant threat throughout the game.

Trivia

  • Chica is voiced by Debi Derryberry in later games.
  • She is one of the original animatronics from the first game.
  • Chica's eyes turn black with white pupils when she is near attacking.
  • She has multiple variations, including Toy Chica and Phantom Chica.
