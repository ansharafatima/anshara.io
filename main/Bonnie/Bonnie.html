Character Overview

Bonnie the Bunny is a prominent antagonist in the Five Nights at Freddy's series. He is an animatronic bunny who plays guitar in Freddy Fazbear's band. Bonnie is known for his menacing appearance and sudden movements.

Appearance

Bonnie is a blue animatronic bunny with red bow tie and guitar. His eyes are usually red, but they turn black with small white pupils when he is in attack mode. His design is sleek but somewhat worn.

Behavior

Bonnie is known for his unpredictable behavior. He often appears in the left side of the security office and can move swiftly through the building. His sudden appearances and attacks make him a constant threat.

Trivia

  • Bonnie is voiced by Joe Gaudet in later games.
  • He is one of the original animatronics from the first game.
  • Bonnie's eyes turn black with white pupils when he is near attacking.
  • He has multiple variations, including Toy Bonnie and Withered Bonnie.
